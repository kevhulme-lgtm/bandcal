# Lineup Beta — Getting Started

Thanks for testing! This is a work in progress so expect rough edges. If something breaks or feels weird, let Kev know.

**https://bandcal.netlify.app**

---

## Signing up

1. Go to **https://bandcal.netlify.app**
2. Pick a username and password — your username is how your bandmates will see you
3. That's it, you're in

---

## Joining the band group

Kev will send you an invite link. Open it, tap **Join**, and you'll land straight in the group calendar.

If you're already signed in and the link says you're already a member, just tap **Open**.

---

## Your personal calendar

This is the **My Calendar** view — it's yours alone, your bandmates only see whether you're free or not, not why.

**Tap any date to cycle through:**
- No colour → you're **available**
- 🔴 Red → **unavailable**
- 🟠 Orange → **tentative** (probably free, not certain)

Tap again to go back to available.

**Long press** a date to add a personal event (gig, holiday, appointment) — this marks the whole date range as unavailable automatically. The event title stays private.

---

## Group view

Switch to **Group View** at the top to see everyone's availability at a glance:

- 🟢 Green — everyone's free
- 🔴 Red — not enough people available
- 🟠 Amber — someone's tentative, or partially available
- **Tap any date** to see exactly who's in and who's out

---

## Group events

In Group View, **long press** a date to add a group event (rehearsal, gig, recording session). This automatically blocks that date for everyone in the group across all their calendars.

When there's a group event on a date, tap it to see the details and mark whether you're available or not.

---

## A few things to know

- Your unavailability syncs across all groups you're in — mark yourself busy once and it shows everywhere
- The calendar updates in real time — no need to refresh
- You can be in multiple groups (useful if you're in more than one band)
- Push notifications will ask permission on first load — worth saying yes so you get notified about new events
